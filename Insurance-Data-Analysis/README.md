**Project Overview**
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes insurance data across multiple policy types including Travel, Health, Auto, Life, and Home. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of premium amounts, claim amounts, coverage, rejection rates, and gender distribution to help understand patterns in insurance claims and policy performance.  

**Dataset**  
**Source:** Kaggle  
**Records:** ~10,000 customers  
**Features:** Policy type, claim status, premium amount, claim amount, coverage amount, gender  
**Used for:** Practice and educational purposes only  

**Key Insights**  
**Travel insurance** had the highest total premium (2.5M) and also the highest claim amount (7.1M), indicating a high-risk policy type  
**Home insurance** had the lowest premium (0.6M) and lowest claim amount (1.6M)  
**Claim vs Premium Gap:** Across all policy types, claim amounts significantly exceed premium amounts, highlighting underpricing risk  
**Rejection Rate:** Home (46) and Life (45) policies have the highest rejection rates, while Auto (42) has the lowest  
**Claim Status:** Out of ~10K claims, 4.36K were Settled, 3.39K were Pending, and 2.26K were Rejected  
**Gender Distribution:** Nearly equal split between Male (5K) and Female (5K) customers  
**Claim Ratio:** Travel and Health have the highest claim ratios (286), while Home has the lowest (264)  


**Insurance Sector Recommendations  
Based on the dashboard insights:**
Reassess Travel insurance premiums as claim amounts (7.1M) are nearly 3x the premium collected (2.5M)  
Investigate high rejection rates in Home and Life policies to improve customer satisfaction and transparency  
Address the large volume of Pending claims (3.39K) to reduce settlement delays  
Explore risk-based pricing models across all policy types to bridge the premium vs claim gap  
Leverage gender and policy-type data to design more personalized insurance products
